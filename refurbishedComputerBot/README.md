# Refurbished Computers Bot V2

### Description
Autofills computer information when entering new refurbished computers by reading from the google sheet!

### How to use it
Upon running the application a window will pop up. Enter the following information:
    1. **Google sheet url** (will be different for each months sheet)
    2. The **row number** of the first computer you want to enter
    3. The **number of computers** you want to autofill

Once you click the *"Run program"* button, an automated chrome browser will open to the ACFS database. You will be prompted to login and navigate to the refurbished computers page. When you're ready, click *"add a new computer"* and watch the magic happen! After you confirm the information looks good you can click *"submit"* and the bot will move on to the next computer.

After autofilling all computers, the program will shut down on its own.

### What not to do
The following are things you probably shouldn't do when using the bot. Note that if the bot ever malfunctions, it will shut down immediately or just stop working entirely. In both these cases you can just close the bot and start it back up again.

- Ensure the information on the spreadsheet is all filled in and the row number is correct.
- The program wil time out after 5 minutes so don't take too long
- Try not to stray too far in the automated browser
